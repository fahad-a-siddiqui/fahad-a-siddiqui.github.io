ProCon is a native iPad app a North American construction contractor runs its
jobsites on, built in Swift and UIKit (MVC + MVVM). It brings the moving parts of
a construction workforce into one place: job and labor-task management, crew
scheduling, tool and asset tracking (check-out, authenticated check-in and
clean-off / RMA returns), employee certifications tied to job roles, OSHA safety
tracking, RFIs, and the reports that hold it all together.
